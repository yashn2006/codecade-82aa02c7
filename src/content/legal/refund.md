# Refund & Cancellation Policy

_Last updated: July 5, 2026_

## 1. Free Trial
Every new café starts with a free trial. No payment is required during the trial and no refund is applicable.

## 2. Cancellation
You may cancel your subscription at any time from **Owner Dashboard → Billing → Cancel plan**. Your access continues until the end of the current paid period.

## 3. Refund Eligibility
- **7-day money-back:** first-time paid subscribers may request a full refund within 7 days of the first successful payment.
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.

## 4. Non-Refundable Cases
- Renewals beyond the 7-day window.
- Add-on charges (SMS credits, extra storage) that have been consumed.
- Accounts suspended for breach of the Terms of Service.
- Third-party fees (payment gateway, taxes) already remitted.

## 5. How to Request
Email **billing@corecade.in** from the account's registered email with:
1. Café name and account email.
2. Payment ID / invoice number.
3. Reason for the refund request.

We acknowledge within 2 business days and resolve within 7 business days.

## 6. Chargebacks
Please contact us before raising a chargeback — most disputes are resolved faster directly.
