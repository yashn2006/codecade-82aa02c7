# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 5, 2026_

CoreCade Technologies ("we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights.

## 1. Data We Collect
- **Account data:** name, email, phone, hashed password, role.
- **Café operational data:** devices, bookings, sessions, orders, memberships, staff, revenue records.
- **Customer data (uploaded by café owners):** name, phone, wallet balance, play history.
- **Payment metadata:** transaction IDs, amount, method (we do not store card numbers).
- **Technical data:** IP address, device/browser, log timestamps, error traces.

## 2. How We Use It
- Deliver, secure and improve the Service.
- Send transactional emails (welcome, invoices, trial expiry, staff invites).
- Detect abuse, comply with law, resolve disputes.

We do **not** sell your personal data.

## 3. Legal Bases (where applicable)
Contract performance, legitimate interest (security, product improvement), consent (marketing), and legal obligation.

## 5. Data Retention
Account and operational data is retained while your subscription is active and for 12 months after cancellation, unless a longer period is required by law (e.g. tax records — 8 years).

## 6. Your Rights
Subject to applicable law (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023), you may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your personal data. Email **privacy@corecade.in** and we will respond within 30 days.

## 7. Security
We use TLS in transit, encryption at rest, row-level security in the database, least-privilege access controls, and audit logging.

## 8. Cookies
We use only strictly-necessary cookies (session, CSRF, preferences). No third-party advertising cookies.

## 9. Children
The Service is not directed to children under 18.
